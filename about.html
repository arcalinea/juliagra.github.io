About Me

I'm a tech activist who learned how to code. I do full stack development, with a focus on the front-end.

I graduated from the University of Pennsylvania in 2013, with a major in Science, Technology, and Society and a concentration in Information Technologies and Organizations. As an activist, I've fought for better internet access for underserved Philadelphia communities, stronger open internet protections, and transparency in international trade agreements that would threaten our rights to connect and communicate.

In my free time, you can probably find me at the local hackerspace, in a place with lots of books, or learning something new on the guitar.
